About Me

Landscape Photographer.

  I am a Mechanical Engineering student with an unconditional love for
mountains and a passion for travelling and
photographing the corners of our world. I grew up in
Tirana, capital of Albania.
  I began appreciating the beauty of the mountains I found
myself in during my early teens and I've been trying to frame
them since.
Those same mountainscapes still don't fail to
inspire me - they can appear so differently depending
on point of view, season or time of day.
  Through photography I attempt to capture
simple moments in a pure fashion. I want to
encourage people to appreciate themselves and their
surroundings more.
